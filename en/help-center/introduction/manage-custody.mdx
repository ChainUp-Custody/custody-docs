ChainUp Custody is an enterprise-level platform focused on providing secure infrastructure for mobile, storage, and issuance of digital assets. Our services target enterprise-level blockchain customer, including exchanges, custodians, banks, trading desks, hedge funds, and Web3 companies. These institutions can securely build, operate, and expand their digital asset operational infrastructure through our custody system.

##  Overview

Our help center guides clients on how to independently set up and use ChainUp Custody's custody system. This enables them to quickly and independently start operations on our platform, minimizing dependence on the ChainUp Custody support team. Clients can access all the functionalities listed in the help center through the app or PC, using features, API, or a combination of both.

##  MPC Wallet Help Guide

<Accordion title="Create and Set Up Your Wallet">
1. Follow the [Create a New MPC Wallet](/quick-start/mpc-wallet/create-wallet) guide to create a new wallet.

2. After creating the wallet, invite other users as your wallet team members to manage the wallet by following the instructions on [Adding New Wallet Member](/help-center/manage-mpc/user-setting/add-user).

3. View the team members of your wallet, and refer to [Roles](/help-center/manage-mpc/user-setting/user-role) for member role permissions.

4. Learn about your wallet's details in [Wallet Architecture](/help-center/manage-mpc/my-wallet/address-format).

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Conduct Asset Management and Wallet Transactions">
1. Examine the [MPC Wallet Asset Dashboard](/help-center/manage-mpc/asset-manage/my-asset) in detail.

2. Manage assets held in your wallet, including [Coin](/help-center/manage-mpc/asset-manage/coin) and [NFT Management](/help-center/manage-mpc/asset-manage/nft).

3. Refer to [MPC Wallet Transactions](/help-center/manage-mpc/transaction/transfer) to deposit and withdraw coins and NFT from your wallet.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Set Up Your Strategy Engine">
1. Review the following articles to build and deploy your strategies: [Transfer Strategy](/help-center/manage-mpc/strategy/transfer).

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Use Web3 Features">
1. Learn how to manage [FIL Multi-Signature Wallets](/help-center/manage-mpc/web3/fil-multi-sign).

2. See the [FIL Node Management](/help-center/manage-mpc/web3/fil-node) article.

3. Understand how to [Connect Your Wallet to DApp](/help-center/manage-mpc/web3/dapp).

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Consolidate Your Wallet Assets">
1. Enable [Contract Consolidation](/help-center/manage-mpc/consolidation/contact/about) or [Co-Signer Consolidation](/help-center/manage-mpc/consolidation/co-signer/about) to consolidate assets scattered across different addresses to a fixed address.

2. Learn how to configure [Contract Consolidation Rules](/help-center/manage-mpc/consolidation/contact/rules) and [Co-Signer Consolidation Rules](/help-center/manage-mpc/consolidation/co-signer/rules).

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Manage Your Wallet Private Key">
1. After creating your wallet, promptly [backup the private key](/quick-start/mpc-wallet/private-manage/private-backup). The MPC private key shares stored on your mobile device are used to sign transactions.

2. If you lose your local private key shares, you can [recover the private key](/quick-start/mpc-wallet/private-manage/private-recover) and sign transactions again. Two recovery methods are supported: manual recovery and cloud recovery.

3. When you want to obtain the complete private key of the MPC wallet, you can refer to [Offline Private Key Recovery](/help-center/manage-mpc/my-wallet/off-line-tool).

</Accordion>
