---
title: Add New Workspace Member
---

If you wish to invite members within your organization to collectively manage your MPC Workspace system, you need to first add them as contacts and then assign them to the MPC Workspace you created.

##  Inviting as Contacts

<Tip>
**To become a contact, users must:**

1. Have a CryptSafe account.

2. Bind and enable Google Authenticator.

</Tip>

<Steps>
<Step title="Enable the Contacts Feature">
    Approach 1: Open the CryptSafe platform in your browser, use the CryptoSafe App, scan the QR code on the homepage or cloud wallet using the "Scan" function, and click on "Contacts" on the list page.

    Approach 2: Log in to the CryptSafe App, click on the profile picture, open account settings, and choose the "Contacts" feature.
</Step>
<Step title="Enter the Account">
    Enter the account (phone number/email) registered with the CryptSafe system.
</Step>
<Step title="Successful Addition">
    When you successfully add a member, you can modify the user's nickname and view the user's role details in different workspaces or delete the user.
</Step>
</Steps>

##  Inviting as an MPC Workspace Ordinary Member

<Tip>
**Note for Inviting MPC Workspace Ordinary Members:**

Only the MPC Workspace private key holder can add/delete workspace members.

When a ordinary  Member joins the transaction approval flow, the member is automatically upgraded to an approver. For adding transaction approval flow, refer to [Wallet Strategy Engine](/help-center/manage-mpc/strategy/transfer).

</Tip>

<Steps>
<Step title="Use Team Management Feature">
    Log in to the CryptoSafe App's MPC Workspace, open the "Security" - "Team Management" feature.
</Step>
<Step title="Select MPC Workspace">
    Select the MPC Workspace you want to manage.
</Step>
<Step title="Add Member as MPC Workspace Ordinary Member">
    From your contact list, choose a user and add them as a ordinary member of the MPC Workspace.
</Step>
</Steps>
