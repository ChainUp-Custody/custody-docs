ChainUp Custody supports multi-signature mechanisms to manage DeFi protocols, preventing malicious modifications to smart contracts.

<Accordion title="MPC Technology Eliminating Single Points of Failure">
ChainUp Custody employs MPC encryption technology, capable of creating and encrypting corresponding private key shares in isolated secure hardware enclaves spanning multiple cloud centers. These key shares are used for executing multi-round computations during transaction signing, eliminating the need to transmit them to the same environment. Consequently, MPC eliminates single points of compromise for private key creation and signing, ensuring higher security and privacy.
</Accordion>
