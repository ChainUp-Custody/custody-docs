---
title: Institutional and Family Office
---
Keysecure assists institutions and family offices in securely managing large-scale digital assets, providing compliance solutions, reducing risks, and simplifying asset management processes. It enables transparent transaction records and audit tracking to meet regulatory requirements. Keysecure's solution is cost-effective, user-friendly, and contributes to the success of institutions and family offices in the digital asset space.

<Accordion title="Enterprise-Grade Security Architecture">
Keysecure employs the latest MPC encryption technology combined with hardware isolation in a multi-layered approach to protect funds from network attacks, internal collusion, and human errors, ensuring the security of clients and investors' capital.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Easy-Integration SDK">
Keysecure wallet features a convenient and user-friendly SDK, facilitating easy-to-use APIs and plug-and-play SDKs. Developers can quickly familiarize themselves with Keysecure services and seamlessly integrate them into their applications, reducing the workload required for wallet development.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Permission Control and Custom Workflow">
Keysecure services provide role-based access control, effectively segregating user permissions based on the scale and needs of client organizations. It allows users to configure batch risk control strategies and transaction whitelists influencing transaction processing and approval methods.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Efficient Financial Reconciliation">
Keysecure offers a clear and visual asset dashboard, professional and comprehensive financial data, and related detailed statements, supporting clients in obtaining real-time asset change data for internal audits and achieving full-path fund monitoring.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="All-In-One Transaction Asset Management">
Keysecure's system platform supports all-in-one co-management of enterprise asset information. A single set of MPC private keys can manage millions of independent wallets efficiently and securely, enabling real-time transaction monitoring and wallet management without the need for repetitive backups.
</Accordion>
