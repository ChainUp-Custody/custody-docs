---
title: NFT Marketplaces
---

ChainUp Custody comprehensively supports all popular NFT main chains in the market, making it easier for the NFT trading market to manage the storage and transactions of NFTs on different main chains, providing greater flexibility and liquidity. Simultaneously, it assists NFT trading markets in complying with asset regulations and regulatory requirements. All features can be seamlessly integrated through a single platform, allowing users to quickly access all applicable services.

<Accordion title="Secure Wallet Architecture">
ChainUp Custody adopts a secure layer of MPC encryption and secure hardware enclaves to protect all potential attack surfaces, avoiding reliance on a single security technology. ChainUp Custody's multi-layer security has been validated in practical applications and continually evolves to prevent external attacks, internal collusion, and human errors from compromising clients.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="KYT and AML Integration">
Collaborating with the industry-leading KYT (Know Your Transaction) digital asset compliance solution, ChainUp Custody provides real-time transaction monitoring directly from the platform. Each transaction is automatically filtered based on your configured compliance policy rules and compliance provider data to prevent sending transactions to risky wallets. The compliance team can also freeze recharge transactions for potential risk wallets, conducting compliance assessments before using funds.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Web3 Command Center">
Effortlessly view, manage, and control every wallet and Web3 transaction for all teams, allowing users to connect in-app wallets to DApps, enabling participation in loyalty programs, earning money games, token-gated activities, and more.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Multi-Chain Support and NFT Standards">
ChainUp Custody supports over 200 main chains and over 1,000 token varieties, while also being compatible with various NFT token standards such as ERC, BEP, SPL, etc. These features meet diverse blockchain needs, providing clients with extensive choices.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Easy-Integration SDK">
ChainUp Custody wallet features a convenient and user-friendly SDK, providing a straightforward API and plug-and-play SDKs. Developers can quickly grasp ChainUp Custody services and easily integrate them into their applications, reducing the workload required for wallet development.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Powerful Policy Engine">
To adapt to your workflow, ChainUp Custody offers customizable user permission features and supports multi-dimensional flexible transaction risk control strategies to ensure asset security.
</Accordion>
