## **Function Introduction**

KYC (Know Your Customer) identity verification is a process that users need to complete before adding team members to ensure account security. KYC supports two different verification identities, individual and business, and users can choose the appropriate verification method based on their type.

## **Operation Procedure**

<Steps>

<Step title="Enter the KYC Verification Page">
Click on the personal avatar in the upper left corner, select KYC verification, and enter the identity verification page.
    <div className="w-full flex items-center justify-center">
    <img src="/images/custody-guide/personalen.png" width="200"/>
</div>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose Verification Identity">
    Supports two different verification identities, individual and business. Choose the appropriate verification method based on user type.
    
    - **Individual Verification:**
        Submit personal ID, photos, and other relevant information.

    - **Business Verification:**
        Submit corporate legal person ID, business license, and other related information.

   <div className="w-full flex items-center justify-center">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
        <img src="/images/quickstart/pkyc.png" width="200"/>
        <img src="/images/quickstart/ekyc.png" width="200"/>
    </CardGroup>
</div>

</Step>

<Step title="Fill in Verification Information">
    Fill in the corresponding verification information based on the chosen verification identity, ensuring the accuracy of the information.

    Upload the required ID, photos, and other verification files.
</Step>

<Step title="Submit Verification Application">
    After confirming that the filled-in verification information is correct, submit the verification application.

    The system will automatically verify the identity information.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for the Review Result">
    Wait for the system review, which is usually completed within a certain number of working days.

    Users can check the verification status in the APP.
</Step>

<Step title="Verification Completed">
    Once the review is approved, the user has completed the KYC identity verification.
</Step>

</Steps>

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

<Accordion title="How do I check my KYC verification status?">
In the APP, click on the personal avatar in the upper left corner, select KYC verification, and you can check the verification status.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does KYC review take?">
KYC review is usually completed within a certain number of working days, the specific time may vary, please be patient and wait.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I modify the submitted KYC verification information?">
Before submitting the verification application, you can modify the filled-in verification information.

Once the application is submitted, it cannot be modified again, and you need to wait for the review result.
</Accordion>


<Accordion title="Do I need to complete KYC verification?">
"If you need to use a personal wallet, you must complete the online KYC vertification; otherwise, trading functions will not be available. The use of corporate wallets is not subject to the online KYC vertification requirement."
</Accordion>
