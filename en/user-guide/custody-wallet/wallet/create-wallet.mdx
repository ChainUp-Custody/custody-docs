## I. Function Introduction

Designed specifically for enterprise users, it supports multiple members and multiple roles to collaborate in managing the wallet, and provides withdrawal strategies and approval functions. As the owner of the wallet, you will have the following permissions:

- **Team Member Management**: Add wallet management team members on the PC end, assign team member duties. Co-managed members (approval and ordinary) can initiate or approve withdrawals in the APP, and view approval progress and fund flow records.
- **Withdrawal Strategy Management**: Formulate withdrawal strategies for important currencies to ensure that withdrawal operations follow the approval process and ensure fund safety.
- **API Withdrawal Address Management**: Manage API withdrawal addresses on the PC end and set withdrawal whitelist addresses.

## II. Operation Procedure

### Create a Wallet

Open the Keysecure official website, download the [Keysecure App](https://custody.Keysecure .com/download/), or directly scan the QR code to download.

<Step title="Complete KYC Certification">
 In the Keysecure wallet, click on the personal avatar in the upper left corner, select KYC certification, and enter the identity certification page.
 KYC review is usually completed within a certain number of working days, the specific time may vary, please be patient.
    <div className="w-full flex items-center justify-center">
    <img src="/images/custody-guide/sercuity.png" width="200"/>
</div>
</Step>


<Step title="Log in to the PC end">
Open the browser and direct to the Keysecure [login page](https://custody.Keysecure .com/login), use the APP to scan the code to authorize login.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a Wallet">
On the web end, click on the [+] in the upper right corner of the page to create a wallet.
