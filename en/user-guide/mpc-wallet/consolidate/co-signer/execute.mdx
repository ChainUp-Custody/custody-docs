## I. Function Introduction

<Tip> 
This function is automatically executed by the system, no manual operation is required.
</Tip> 

Co-Signer consolidation execution is a convenient and automated function designed to help you manage digital assets efficiently.

By configuring the Co-Signer's API and consolidation rules, the system will regularly scan asset amounts according to your rules and execute consolidation operations, without the need for manual participation, achieving automatic centralized management of assets.

Through Co-Signer consolidation execution, we are committed to providing you with a smarter and more efficient digital asset management experience, ensuring that your assets are effectively centralized and managed.


## II. Operation Procedure

<Steps>
<Step title="Configure API and Consolidation Rules">
   After completing the configuration of the Co-Signer's API and consolidation rules, the system will perform asset scanning and consolidation operations according to your settings.
   
   For the specific configuration process, please refer to [Configuring Co-Signer Rules](/help-center/manage-mpc/consolidation/co-signer/rules).
</Step>
<Step title="Regularly Execute Consolidation">
    The Co-Signer will regularly scan the asset amounts according to the rules you set and automatically execute the consolidation operation to ensure that the assets are efficiently centralized to the target address.

</Step>
<Step title="View Order Information in Real Time">
    You can view the transaction information of the consolidation order and refueling order in real time through the App or Web.
    
    This information is classified under the transfer type, allowing you to keep track of the flow of assets at any time.
