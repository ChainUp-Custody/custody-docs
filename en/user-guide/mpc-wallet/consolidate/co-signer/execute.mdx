##  Function Introduction

<Tip> 
This function is automatically executed by the system, no manual operation is required.
</Tip> 

The Sweep All Coins Feature is a convenient and automated function designed to help you manage digital assets efficiently.

By configuring the Co-Signer's API and auto sweeping rules, the system will regularly scan asset amounts according to your rules and execute sweeping operations, without the need for manual participation, achieving automatic centralized management of assets.

Through API Co-Signer sweeping execution, we are committed to providing you with a smarter and more efficient digital asset management experience, ensuring that your assets are effectively centralized and managed.


##  Operation Procedure

<Steps>
<Step title="Configure API and Auto Sweeping Rules">
   After completing the configuration of the Co-Signer's API and auto sweeping rules, the system will perform asset scanning and sweeping operations according to your settings.
   
   For the specific configuration process, please refer to [Configuring Auto Sweeping Rules](/help-center/manage-mpc/consolidation/co-signer/rules).
</Step>
<Step title="Regularly Sweeping Assets">
    The auto sweep system will regularly scan the asset amounts according to the rules you set and automatically execute the sweeping operation to ensure that the assets are efficiently centralized to the target address.

</Step>
<Step title="View Order Information in Real Time">
    You can view the transaction information of the auto sweeping order and auto fueling order in real time through the App or Web.
    
    This information is classified under the transfer type, allowing you to keep track of the flow of assets at any time.
    


</Step>
</Steps>
