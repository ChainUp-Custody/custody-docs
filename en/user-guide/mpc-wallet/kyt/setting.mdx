## I. Function Introduction

KYT (Know Your Transaction) is a financial regulation and compliance practice designed to help financial institutions and other organizations identify and monitor suspicious transactions related to money laundering, terrorist financing, and other illegal activities. By monitoring transaction data in real-time or near real-time, KYT aims to identify abnormal or suspicious transaction behavior to ensure the compliance of the financial system.

ChainUp Custody supports three leading and well-known KYT service providers in the industry. Users can freely choose based on their business needs and cost considerations, and they can also freely switch during operation.

**Elliptic:** Focused on providing cryptocurrency compliance and risk management solutions, widely used by large financial and regulatory institutions around the world.

**Trustformer:** Independently developed by ChainUp Group, providing leading digital currency compliance monitoring solutions to ensure the compliance and security of the digital asset ecosystem.

<warning>
**Note:** KYT includes the following states:
1. KYT Verification: This state includes two different situations:
    - Your business has already created a KYT order and submitted it to the KYT service provider for verification, at this time you just need to wait for the KYT service provider to return the verification result;
    - Your business has submitted the KYT service provider for verification, but the KYT service verification has timed out, at this time a KYT verification timeout to-do will be generated for you;
2. KYT Manual Processing: If your configured receipt processing method is "Manual Processing", at this time the deposit order reaches your configured alarm level, the order status is KYT manual processing, and you need to confirm the processing of this to-do. If this to-do is not processed, to ensure the safety of funds, the coin of this wallet will be blocked from withdrawal and consolidation.
3. KYT Rejection: If your configured withdrawal processing method is "Refuse Withdrawal", at this time the withdrawal order reaches your configured alarm level, then the order will be directly refused to withdraw, and this status is the final state of the order;
</warning>

## III. Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Why do I need to configure KYT services?">
Configuring KYT services is to enhance the security of the account, perform secondary verification with KYT to prevent unauthorized access.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I backup KYT service configuration information?">
You can backup KYT service configuration information by taking screenshots or writing it down by hand. In case of device replacement or loss of KYT service configuration information, you can use the backup information for recovery.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Which service providers does KYT service support?">
Currently, Custody supports KYT services provided by the following three KYT service providers: Trustformer, Chainalysis, and Elliptic.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does KYT service handle risky transactions?">
KYT service will alert risky transactions and handle them according to your configuration. For example, if your configured receipt processing method is "Manual Processing", at this time the deposit order reaches your configured alarm level, the order status is KYT manual processing, and you need to confirm the processing of this to-do. If this to-do is not processed, to ensure the safety of funds, the coin of this wallet will be blocked from withdrawal and consolidation.
</Accordion>
