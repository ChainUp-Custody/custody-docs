##  Function Introduction

In the ChainUp Custody workspace, you can receive two types of assets: tokens and NFTs. You can select the crypto coin or NFT on the asset page to view the receiving address.

##  Operation Process

<Steps>
<Step title="Select the Type of Asset to Receive">
According to your business needs, choose to receive Tokens or NFTs to the selected wallets.
</Step>
<Step title="View Receiving Address">
In the **APP**, Tokens support quick viewing of the receiving address by swiping left, or you can click to enter the crypto coin details to view the receiving address. NFTs only support viewing the receiving address by clicking to enter details.

<Tip>
Asset Security Tips:

1. You can confirm the authenticity of the transfer coin through the contract address;

2. walletss of the web3 type receive payments in real-time, while asset-type walletss need to reach a certain number of confirmations to receive payments.
</Tip>

##  Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Why can't I initiate the receiving of crypto coin or NFT ?">
Please confirm the following points:

1. Have you enabled the network for the crypto coin or NFT ?
2. Have you followed the crypto coin or NFT? Please note, information about unfollowed currencies/NFTs will be hidden;
3. Is the crypto coin or NFT undergoing a blockchain upgrade?

If you are still unable to receive payments, please contact online customer service for help.
</Accordion>
