##  Function Introduction

In the ChainUp Custody wallet, you can view and handle to-do tasks related to your account. The type of to-do tasks you have will depend on your account role in the selected main wallet.

##  Operation Procedure

<Steps>
<Step title="View To-Do Information">
In the **APP**, you can enter the [To-Do] page through the bottom navigation to view and handle all wallet to-do information you manage.
<div className="w-full flex items-center justify-center">
    <CardGroup cols={3}>
    <img src="/images/user-guide/todo1.png" width="200"/>
    <img src="/images/user-guide/todo2.png" width="200"/>
    <img src="/images/user-guide/todo3.png" width="200"/>
    </CardGroup>
</div>

<Tip>
You can select the wallet by clicking on the upper right of the [To-Do] page.
</Tip>

On the **Web Page**, click on the "Operational To-Do" module in the left menu bar to jump to the function page.
<div className="w-full flex items-center justify-center">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/todo4.png" />
</div>
<Tip>
You can only view, select, and operate the to-do tasks associated with your account under the currently selected main wallet.
</Tip>

</Step>
<Step title="Handle Operational To-Do">
The type of to-do tasks you can operate depends on your account role in the selected main wallet.

**Approval members** can carry out transaction approval, agree or refuse transactions, confirm or cancel the collection and gas wallet configuration.

**Wallet creators** can carry out transaction cancellation, transaction signature, transaction acceleration, etc.

<div className="w-full flex items-center justify-center">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/todo5.png" />
</div>


<Warning>
Please note the following two points:
1. When operating on the MPC wallet platform, transaction approval requires GA verification.
2. For any operation that requires a private key signature, you can use the ChainUp Custody App with a locally backed-up private key to scan the QR code.
</Warning>
</Step>
</Steps>

##  Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How do I view the operational to-do of other main wallets?">
You can click the MPC button at the top of the page, or click the main wallet switch button on the left, and after switching to another main wallet, you can enter the operational to-do module of that main wallet to view.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why can't I complete tasks waiting for signature and acceleration by scanning the QR code of the to-do?">
Please confirm the following points:
1. You are the creator of the main wallet;
2. The device you are using has completed the local backup of the private key;
3. The QR code has not expired.

If you still can't perform the scanning operation, please contact online customer service for help.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why can't I view Fil-related to-do tasks?">
Sorry, we temporarily do not support users to perform Fil-related to-do operations on the MPC wallet Web platform. You can view and handle Fil-related to-do tasks through the Custody APP.
</Accordion>
